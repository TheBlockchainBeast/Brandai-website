Frequently Asked Question

What is BRANDAI and what services does it offer?

BRANDAI is a mobile army knife crypto marketing agency offering a range of AI-powered services. Our Telegram bot can generate websites, smart contracts, whitepapers, GIFs, and logos based on user input, catering to the needs of both crypto enthusiasts and newcomers.

How does the BRANDAI Telegram bot work for different functionalities?

Our Telegram bot utilizes various AI models for different functionalities. For website generation, we use AI-powered builders or templates. Smart contracts are created using Solidity and blockchain platforms like Ethereum. Deep learning models or generative algorithms are employed for GIFs and logos, while AI algorithms generate text content for whitepapers.

What is BRANDAI's mission in the cryptocurrency world?

BRANDAI aims to empower both "degens" (crypto veterans) and "normies" (newcomers) in cryptocurrency. Our mission is to provide a seamless, efficient platform for users to create their own crypto projects with our advanced bot features.

Who is the target audience of BRANDAI's services?

BRANDAI focuses on delivering a user-friendly experience for both experienced crypto enthusiasts and newcomers. We aim to make participation in the crypto world accessible to everyone, regardless of their technical background.

What is the $BRAND token, and what is its total supply?

The $BRAND token is BRANDAI's native token, with a total supply of 1,000,000 tokens. It features a 5% transaction tax to support various aspects of the project's ecosystem.

How is the transaction tax on $BRAND token utilized?

The 5% transaction tax on the $BRAND token is distributed for Revenue Share (50%), Marketing and Development (25%), and the Liquidity Pool (LP) (25%). This structure is designed to motivate long-term holding, support project growth, and ensure a stable trading environment.

How does the Revenue Share from $BRAND token tax benefit users?

50% of the $BRAND token tax goes to Revenue Share, encouraging long-term holding and active participation among token holders, aligning with the community's interests.

What benefits does the Liquidity Pool allocation from $BRAND token tax offer?

Allocating 25% of the $BRAND token tax to the Liquidity Pool (LP) ensures a stable and seamless trading environment on decentralized exchanges, enhancing the overall liquidity of the $BRAND token.
